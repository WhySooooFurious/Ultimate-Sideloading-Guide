Before We Start

Ensure you have not used services like Scarlet before, as the certificates used by me will be revoked. This method works as long as all steps are followed, and the DNS is never disabled, which is crucial.

Step 1: Installing the WhySooooFurious DNS Blocker

Note: I will eventually add visual aids in the guide itself.

  1. Go to Downloads and click on the WhySooooFurious DNS Blocker. Allow the download and close the prompt.
  2. Open Settings, go to General > VPN & Device Management.
  3. Click on the downloaded profile and install it. You may need to input your password.
  4. The DNS should now be automatically enabled. Do not change the DNS to automatic, or your apps will get revoked!
  5. Go to General > About > Certificate Trust Settings.
  6. Enable full trust for the NextDNS Root Certificate.

Continue to Part 2 if you want to install ESign.

Step 2: Installing ESign

Note: Visual aids will be added later.

  1. Go to Downloads and click on "ESign Direct Install".
  2. Click on install when the popup appears.
  3. Open Settings, go to General > VPN & Device Management.
  4. Select the HDFC app and click on Trust. Then click on Allow.
  5. Your device may need to restart. If it restarts, swipe up and install the profile.
  6. ESign should now be on your home screen, fully functional.
